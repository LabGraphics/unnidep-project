OUR GOALS:

The objectives of UNNIDEP are to engage and galvanize communities across the United States to support and implement programs in the three basic areas of healthcare, education, and agriculture across Nimba County, Liberia, West Africa.

We intend to achieve the above by:

  • Engaging in infrastructural development initiatives through reconstructing, renovating, and undertaking developmental programs to improve infrastructures such as schools, clinics, libraries, agriculture, farming, safe drinking water, roads, etc. in Nimba County;


  • Enhancing literacy experiences through procuring and shipping books and other educational materials to enhance literacy experiences at schools and libraries in Nimba County;


  • Improving student enrollment and retention through financial assistance and support by funding programs that provide scholarship and financial assistance to students from Nimba County who are studying at institutions of high learning across Liberia and students who are attending in the county;


  • Incentivizing educators in communities by undertaking programs that would help subsidize the salaries of teachers within Nimba County;


  • Augmenting medical supplies and equipment to communities by undertaking programs to supplement the availability of medical supplies and equipment for use in clinics and dispensaries within Nimba County;


  • Enriching community experiences and cohesiveness through shared facilities by way of assisting with the construction or enhancement of public facilities such as town halls, market places, community centers, and restrooms;


  • Improving agricultural productivity through learning by undertaking programs that would assist local farmers in learning new farming methods to increase productivity and improve the harvest of cash and food crops;


  • Undertaking self-help projects for empowerment through spearheading projects that will enhance capacity building and advance economic, social, and financial empowerment in Nimba County.
